Manage Ads

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in our app. Depending on your region, you may be shown a consent message asking whether you want to receive personalized ads:

  • European Economic Area (EEA) & United Kingdom: You will see a consent form where you can choose between personalized ads or non-personalized ads.
  • California, USA: You will see a consent message in line with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).
  • Other regions: A consent form may not appear. In these cases, Google manages your ad settings by default.

Even if the consent form does not appear in your region, you can still manage ad personalization through your device settings:

  • On iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising > Personalized Ads.
  • On Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization.

This ensures you always have control over whether you receive personalized ads or more generic ads.
